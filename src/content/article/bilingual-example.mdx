This post demonstrates the optional bilingual feature. Set `bilingual: true` in the frontmatter and a small **EN / 中文** toggle appears under the title.

Write the English (or first language) version first. Then drop in a marker `<div id="zh-start"></div>` and write the Chinese (or second language) version below it. The runtime splits the article on that marker and shows one side at a time.

You can also provide `titleZh` and `subtitleZh` in frontmatter — the title swaps when the toggle is clicked.

The user's choice is remembered in `localStorage` under `preferred-lang`, so they see the same language on subsequent posts.
